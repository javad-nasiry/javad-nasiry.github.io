About

I am a Full Professor in the Operations Management area at the Desautels Faculty of Management, McGill University. I serve as Director, Sustainable Growth Initiative (SGI). My work sits at the intersection of behavioral operations, supply chain management, and sustainability, with applications in healthcare and retail.

  • Director, Sustainable Growth Initiative (SGI)
  • Ph.D., INSEAD
  • Previously on faculty at HKUST (2011–2019)

McGill Profile Email Google Scholar

Research Interests

Context: Behavioral Operations Management, Supply Chain Management, Sustainability, Healthcare Operations, Operations–Marketing Interface, Operations–Finance Interface.

Methods: Optimization, Game Theory, Dynamic Programming, Mechanism Design; Experimental Design, Statistical Analysis, Structural Estimation.
