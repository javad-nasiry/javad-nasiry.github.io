About

I am a Full Professor in the Operations Management area at the Desautels Faculty of Management, McGill University. My work sits at the intersection of behavioral operations, supply chain management, and sustainability, with applications in healthcare and retail.

  • Director (former), Sustainable Growth Initiative (SGI)
  • Ph.D., INSEAD
  • Previously on faculty at HKUST (2011–2019)

McGill Profile Email Google Scholar

Research Interests

Context: Behavioral Operations Management, Supply Chain Management, Sustainability, Healthcare Operations, Operations–Marketing, Operations–Finance.

Methods: Optimization, Game Theory, Dynamic Programming, Mechanism Design; Experimental Design, Statistical Analysis, Structural Estimation.
